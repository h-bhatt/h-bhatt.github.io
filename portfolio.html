CC3 - Use a visualisation in economic/policy commentary.

I'm discussing the question "Trading nation: How can we get Britain exporting again?" which was one of the key themes at the Festival of Economics 2024.

The UK's exports have consistently increased. Currently, it is 13th largest exporting nation in the world. However, it used to have much high export share in world trade.

The UK's exports are rising but remain insufficient to offset its persistently negative trade balance. Aligning export strategies with GDP growth and addressing trade barriers is critical.
